John Lennon Fatally Shot Outside New York Residence

April 17, 2026 • New York City • EchoNews Live Desk

New York — Music icon John Lennon has been shot and killed outside his Manhattan residence late Friday evening in what authorities are calling a targeted attack.

According to preliminary police reports, Lennon was approached by a hooded individual as he returned home at approximately 3:40 PM. Multiple shots were fired at close range.

Emergency responders arrived within minutes, but Lennon was pronounced dead shortly after arrival at a nearby hospital.

“We are treating this as a homicide. A suspect is in custody and there is no ongoing threat to the public.” — NYPD Statement

Police Report Summary

  • Time of incident: 8:47 PM
  • Location: Upper West Side, Manhattan
  • Suspect: Male, apprehended on scene
  • Weapon recovered at scene
  • Motive under investigation

Arrest Details

Law enforcement sources confirm the suspect did not attempt to flee and was taken into custody without resistance. Later identified as Mark David Chapman. Investigators are reviewing surveillance footage and interviewing witnesses.

Witness Accounts

Several witnesses reported hearing “four to five rapid gunshots” followed by screams. One bystander described the scene as “surreal,” stating the suspect remained standing calmly before police arrived.
